Digital Technique - Poster Art

For the month of January the Digital Technique is to transform a photograph into poster art using the Cutout Filter.
1. Size a color image to 1024 x 768, 72 dpi, as per usual.
2. Use the cutout filter, under Filter > Artistic to create a poster effect. I use values of 7, 1, and 2.
3. Saturate the colors. Go to Image > Adjustment> Hue Saturation Brightness. Move the Saturation Sider to about 45.
4. Sharpen the file. Choose Filter>unsharp mask. Try values like 115, 1, 1. Gives the colors a crisper edge, separating them from each other better.
If your end results resembles a paint by number picture, you have done well.

I have attached a before and after sample showing a photo transformed into poster art. You'll find out this technique looks better on some photos more than others. You'll find out which images work and which ones don't as you experiment with your own photos. Have fun and if your menus are slightly different in your software program then play and experiment - you don't have to use my exact directions as long as you have fun and get results you like. If you want to add text as a finishing touch on your posterized photo, that's fine too - the sky's the limit!
