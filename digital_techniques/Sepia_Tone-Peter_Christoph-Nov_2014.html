SEPIA TONE PRINT
Here are the instructions to convert a photo into what resembles an old fashioned sepia tone print, using photoshop. From the menu, choose Image>Adjust>Hue/Saturation. In the dialog box that appears, first check the colorize box, and for Hue, choose 35, for Saturation choose 25, for lightness choose 0. If you are using a different software program, the locations of your menu items may vary. You should get something that resembles my sample photo as far as color. Use a photo which you might imagine could have been taken over 100 years ago.
Have fun! And yes that is the only step necessary to create your sepia tone print!
