Competition

Our monthly slide competition consists of digital slides in four categories:

  • Pictorial
  • Nature
  • Monochrome
  • Digital Technique
  • Assigned Subjects

The print competition, will be at the last meeting of the year, with three categories:

  • Black & white
  • Creative
  • Color

Size of images for competition, should be no larger than 1920x1440. That is, a maximum width of 1920 pixels and a maximum height of 1440 pixels.

Submissions

Images must be submitted no later than Noon (12:30pm) on the day of the competition.

We use Photo Club Services for submitting and managing our competitions: Competition Website
